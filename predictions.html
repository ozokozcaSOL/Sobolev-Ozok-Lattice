Cosmology

Source: “From Pre-Geometric Coherence to the Present Universe…” (Zenodo: 16890398).

Discrete step-count signatures (testable)

  • BAO: small, coherent phase offsets tied to the step-count discretization.
  • FRBs: small frequency-independent (achromatic) propagation delays distinguishable from plasma dispersion.
  • Standard sirens: possible GW–EM luminosity distance mismatches if the channels couple differently to the lattice.

Black holes

Source: “Black Holes from SOL: Derivation, … & Quasi-Black-Hole Signatures” (Zenodo: 16889440).

QBH “discovery plot” (strong falsifiability)
  • A strict linear relation between shadow deviation and ISCO shift: δsh = s(q) · δISCO with discrete slopes s(q) = 3, 6, 12 for q = 1, 2, 3.
  • Systems nearer the occupation threshold (low σ/σmin) should show larger deviations than high-dispersion systems.
