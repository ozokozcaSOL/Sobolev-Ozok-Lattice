Sobolev–Ozok Lattice (SOL)

An exploratory framework for coherence, resolution, and emergent structure in fundamental physics.

Research Philosophy

I am allowed to explore a profound idea without needing it to be ultimate, accepted, or correct.

This work approaches theoretical physics as an exploratory process rather than a search for final answers. Its aim is to investigate whether familiar physical laws, probabilities, and observational outcomes may arise from deeper structural constraints on what configurations of the universe can persist.

Uncertainty is treated not as a weakness, but as an essential component of honest inquiry. Historically, progress in physics has often begun with tentative frameworks that clarified the space of possible explanations long before experimental or communal consensus emerged.

The models presented here are not assertions of ultimate truth. They are proposals intended to be examined, challenged, refined, or discarded as understanding advances.

Status: Active theoretical research program. Manuscripts archived on Zenodo and under continued development.
